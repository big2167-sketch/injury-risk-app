# App Testing Notes

- Confirmed the app loads tracking_sample.csv
- Confirmed football tracking rows are removed
- Confirmed speed is converted from yards/second to mph

## Limitations
The app does not include confirmed injury labels. Therefore, the risk category is a proxy based on movement intensity rather than a confirmed injury prediction.
